Link scrap → Plain text item

The strictest interpretation of "this is a shopping list now." A link scrap becomes a plain grocery row — just the title, no thumbnail — so the list stays visually uniform and scannable while shopping. The original link isn't lost: it survives as a small tap-through 🔗 chip on the row, so you can still open the recipe if you need it.
