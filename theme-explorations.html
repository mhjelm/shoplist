Three Worlds
For Shoplist

Exploring tonal directions beyond light, dark, and the current playful shoplist palette. Each theme below is a distinct atmosphere — palette, typography, motion, and ornament moving in the same direction. Every theme also defines six core micro-interactions: tap-to-check, undo, add, empty state, "new" marker, and the loading transition.
