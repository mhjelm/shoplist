Design A · of 3

Link scrap → Thumbnail item

When a Scrapbook becomes a shopping list, a link scrap collapses into an ordinary grocery row: its title is the item name and the unfurled og:image fills the normal item-photo slot. No screenshot needed — we already grabbed the page image at unfurl time. The link itself goes dormant (still stored, just not shown). Reads exactly like a hand-added item with a photo. Two ways in: convert the list in place (below) or copy selected scraps into another list.
