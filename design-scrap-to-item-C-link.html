Link scrap → Link-preserving item

The richest option: a former link scrap keeps its identity inside the shopping list — title, thumbnail, host pill, and a tap-to-open link — while still being a checkable shopping row. Best when the family shops from recipes: you can open the page mid-aisle to double-check an ingredient. The trade-off is a busier list where recipe cards sit alongside plain food rows.
