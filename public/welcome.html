Recipe & list import

Three ways in.

  • 🔗 From a link — recipe URLs are fetched and parsed automatically.
  • 📷 From a photo — snap a handwritten list or a recipe page and the vision model reads it.
  • ⌨️ From text — paste anything; quantities and units are understood (“2 förp à 250 g”).
  • 📲 From anywhere — share a link or image into Shoplist straight from another app.
Auto-sorted

Your list, already in shopping order.

Add “halloumi” and it quietly files itself under Mejeri. Add “tvål” and it’s Hygien. Items are tagged across eleven aisles automatically, remembered for next time, and grouped so you walk the store once — not back and forth.

  • ✓ Smart category guessing on every add
  • ✓ Learns your corrections and reuses them
  • ✓ Drag the aisles into your store’s order in Settings

Tap into store mode and everything else fades away: oversized rows you can hit one-handed with a basket on your arm, and a screen wake-lock so your phone never dims halfway down the aisle.
